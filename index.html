Grill on the Square is Leeds' favourite spot for all things steak and cocktails.



Warm and cosy in the winter, open and breezy in the summer. There are two outdoor dining areas - one at the front looking out to East Parade, the other an all year round conservatory looking out onto Bond Square and the Dakota hotel, with heaters to keep it toasty, and a retractable roof when we fancy going al-fresco.
